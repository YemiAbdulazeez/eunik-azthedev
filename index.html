About Us What we do?

A little history...

Eunik, formerly known as EunikFits Clothing started as a Fashion brand in 2018 which was founded by Olasedidun Olamide. The brand continued its evolution and ever since then remain consistent, competitive and fast rising amongst her contemporaries even in the fashion industry. By 2022, it transitioned to a full fledge brand with accomplishments like:

  • Having more subdivisions namely real estate and food.
  • Official Commissioning of its Mokola Office space in Ibadan, Oyo State.
  • Launching of its very own fashion school.
